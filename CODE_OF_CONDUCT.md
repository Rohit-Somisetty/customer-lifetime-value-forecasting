# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free environment. Contributors, maintainers, and stakeholders are expected to:

- Use respectful, inclusive language.
- Consider the diverse background of marketing, data, and engineering partners.
- Provide helpful, actionable feedback.
- Accept constructive criticism graciously.

Unacceptable behavior includes discrimination, harassment, personal attacks, or publishing private information without consent.

If you witness or experience a violation, please contact the maintainer at support@example.com. The maintainer may take any action deemed appropriate, including temporary or permanent bans.
